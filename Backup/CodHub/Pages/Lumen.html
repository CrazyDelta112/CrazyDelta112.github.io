LUMEN ARCHIVE

The definitive Call of Duty build archive. Digital preservation, technical research, version comparison and historical documentation of modern COD titles.

Project Overview

  • What is Lumen? Lumen is a long-term archival and research project dedicated to preserving rare, internal, public and retail Call of Duty builds.
  • Mission To prevent historically important builds from being lost, while documenting the real evolution of engines, systems, UI, gameplay and content.
  • Target Audience Developers, modders, reverse engineers, digital archivists and technical enthusiasts.
